15 diagnostic modules. No false certainty.

Most tools stop at a pass-rate. Candor runs your real history through fifteen independent engines — the kind of analysis that would take a quant weeks to build by hand. This is what’s inside.

MOD/KILLER-RULELIVE

Killer Rule Autopsy

The single rule most likely to end your challenge — and the exact mechanism.

MOD/LEVERAGE-MAPLIVE

Cross-Firm Leverage Map

The blocker with the widest impact across firms — and whether rulesets create opposite failure modes.

MOD/FUNDED-SURVIVALLIVE

Funded Survival

Passing is the easy part. How long your pattern holds the funded account — and whether you reach a first payout before busting.

MOD/CONSISTENCY-CAPLIVE

Consistency Cap Test

Many firms cap how much of your profit one day can carry. Shows whether you’d hit the target and still be eliminated for it.

MOD/SELF-VALIDATIONLIVE

Self-Validation Split

The model grades its own homework: learns on half your history, tests on the half it never saw. Tells you if the odds are real or a hot streak.

MOD/SEQUENCELIVE

Run-of-the-Cards Stress Test

Same days, shuffled order. Reveals if a bad sequence breaks you when the average says you’re safe.

MOD/COMPOUND-TRAPLIVE

Compound Trap Detector

How two or three rules combine into a vise that no single rule reveals on its own.

MOD/REGIMELIVE

Regime Shift Radar

Whether your edge is improving or decaying right now — and how much to trust a single number.

MOD/SIZINGLIVE

Sizing Pressure Lens

Where your sizing sits relative to your own edge — and where overbetting quietly raises ruin risk.

MOD/CONFIDENCELIVE

Adaptive Confidence Calibration

Odds as honest ranges that sharpen as you add data — never a fake 0% or 100%.

MOD/PATH-ENGINELIVE

Streak-Aware Path Engine

Simulation that preserves your real losing and winning runs instead of averaging them away.

MOD/FEE-BURNLIVE

Expected Fee Burn

The real cost of the whole retry loop — not just the first attempt.

MOD/RISK-DNALIVE

Risk DNA

Your behavioral signature under pressure: streaks, concentration, drift.

MOD/WHAT-IFLIVE

What-if Lab

How scaling your risk up or down moves your odds — and your killer rule.
