01Nature of the Product

Candor sells access to digital reports generated from data you upload. Because reports are produced on demand from your own input, they are inherently personalized and digital.

02Free Preview

The free preview is available at no cost and is not eligible for any refund.

03Paid Reports

Once a paid report has been generated and delivered, sales are generally final.

Refunds may be considered in the following situations:

  • A technical failure prevents your report from being generated or delivered.
  • You were charged in error (duplicate charge, wrong amount).
  • The report is materially inaccessible due to our fault.
